Dornoch is an ancient small town with settlements dating back 4000 years

Golf has been played across the world’s most northerly championship links for over four centuries. Entwined in history, at Royal Dornoch the game is inseparable from the ancient Highlands town it belongs to.

The Royal Dornoch Golf Club, located in Scotland, is considered one of the most prestigious and oldest clubs in the world. Founded in 1877, it offers a unique experience of playing in fields marked by natural beauty and rich history.

Courses for beginners

Rent golf clubs

Absolutely! We offer a variety of golf clubs for rent, catering to players of different levels and preferences. Just let us know what you need.

Dress code

Yes, we do. We kindly ask our guests to wear collared shirts, golf shoes, and avoid denim. Please check our detailed dress code guidelines on-site for more information.
